Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, timely, and customer-focused IT support that resolves technical issues efficiently and enhances the user experience. By continuously improving our service processes and communication, we aim to ensure every user receives the best possible support to keep their work uninterrupted and productive.

Vision

Our vision is to become a proactive, data-driven help desk that empowers users through self-service and innovation, positioning the IT support team as a trusted partner in achieving organizational excellence by 2026.

SMART Goals for Fiscal Year 2025

Goal 1: Improve First Contact Resolution Rate

Increase the First Contact Resolution (FCR) Rate by 8% by December 31, 2025, through improved troubleshooting guides, peer-shadowing, and an efficient knowledge base system.

User Benefit: Users receive faster, one-call solutions that minimize downtime and frustration.

Goal 2: Reduce Incident Resolution Time for Priority 2 Issues

Decrease the Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR) for major incidents by 12% by December 31, 2025, through optimized escalation procedures and remote diagnostic permissions for Level 1 technicians.

User Benefit: Reduced business disruptions and quicker restoration of critical services.

Goal 3: Improve Customer Satisfaction Scores

Increase Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) scores by 5% by December 31, 2025, through quarterly soft-skill training, proactive callbacks, and feedback-based improvements.

User Benefit: Users experience more empathetic and responsive support interactions.

Goal 4: Improve Knowledge Base Utilization

Boost successful end-user knowledge base article usage by 15% and technician usage by 10% by December 31, 2025, through promotion campaigns, rewards, and optimized search functionality.

User Benefit: Users gain easier access to quick self-service solutions and reduced ticket submission time.

Goal 5: Improve Technician Compliance with SLAs

Increase the percentage of tickets resolved within SLA timelines from 85% to 92% by December 31, 2025, using real-time SLA dashboards and targeted technician training.

User Benefit: Users experience consistent, dependable service delivery with fewer delays.
